# Content Operations Plan

## Publishing cadence

- 2 SEO posts per month minimum (start)
- 1 service/location landing page per week (growth phase)
- 1 case study per month

## Content themes

1. Event planning education
2. Booking checklists
3. Fundraiser outcomes and playbooks
4. Behind-the-scenes/show highlights

## Editorial standards

- Every article targets one primary intent keyword
- Add internal links to at least 3 relevant pages
- Include one conversion CTA module per article
- Add FAQ section for long-tail search capture

## Governance

- Monthly content planning meeting
- Quarterly content pruning and refresh
- KPI review tied to assisted conversions
